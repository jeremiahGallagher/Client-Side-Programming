Cookies

Cookies allow a website to save preferences and other identifying information pertaining to a particular user. Although this provides better functionality, speed and usability, it also provides a means for someone to access the users information if the cookies are not used correctly. For example, a cookie that stores a users personal contact information such as address and phone number can be a cause for concern if the information is stored locally and not secured on a server or behind another means of protection / data security. Because of this, it is important that the site administration be upfront about any information the site plans to store bout the user. This transparency will provide a sense of trust with the users.
